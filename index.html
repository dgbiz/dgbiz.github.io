About Skin Research Lab

The laboratory of the Korean Association of Skin Analysis and Analytics (KASAA) is a premier research institute located in Seoul, South Korea. Led by Dustin Lee, the Chairman of KASAA, our mission is to enhance the understanding of skin health through multidisciplinary approaches that combine conventional clinical skin analysis methodologies with novel database skin analytics methodologies.

Established with the intent to provide the public with accurate and trustworthy skincare information, we continually push the boundaries of research, ensuring that the data we disseminate is both rigorous and relevant.

Our Services

We are dedicated to advancing the field of dermatology by merging traditional methods with cutting-edge technologies. Our services include:

Clinical Skin Analysis

This service provides an in-depth examination of the skin's health and conditions. Using advanced diagnostic tools and technologies, we assess various skin parameters such as hydration, pigmentation, elasticity, and more. This helps in understanding the skin's current state and any potential issues.

Advanced Skin Database Analytics

We harness the power of big data to delve deep into skin analytics. Using our expansive database, we can identify patterns, correlations, and insights that are not evident through traditional methods. This approach aids in predicting skin trends, understanding underlying skin health concerns, and formulating better skincare strategies.

Public Awareness and Education Initiatives

Recognizing the importance of public awareness in skincare, we organize workshops, seminars, and online webinars to educate individuals about skin health, preventive measures, and the latest in skincare research. Our initiatives are aimed at promoting a more informed and skin-conscious public.

Collaborative Research Opportunities

We collaborate with dermatologists, research institutions, and skincare companies around the globe to drive innovation in the field. By pooling our collective knowledge and resources, we aim to stay at the forefront of skincare research and development.
